What is StudyFlash?

StudyFlash is a web app built around a simple idea: most study tools focus on tracking grades, but the real challenge is learning how to study better in the first place. Instead of just organizing your notes or calculating your GPA, StudyFlash helps you actually learn.

The app is designed around techniques that work ‚Äî active recall, spaced repetition, and building consistent habits. You organize what you want to learn, work through focused study sessions, and see your progress over time. The goal isn't passive rereading; it's intentional practice that sticks.

StudyFlash gives you clear feedback on where you're improving and where you need more work. It helps you spot patterns in how you study, so you can adjust and get better results. The interface is clean and distraction-free, keeping you focused on what matters: actually understanding the material.
